ABOUT RADIX ESPORTS LTD.

Radix eSports LTD. is a UK based eSports organisation operating across all major gaming platforms acting as a base to help grow and progress eSports within the UK.

Founded on the 26th of January 2016 by company direct, Michael "afro" Todner, we are quickly growing throughout the UK scene and hope to be active for years to come.
